Introduction to the task

These datasets contain information about a randomised sample of 10K anonymised business customers and their bill information. The datasets include all bills for each customer, plus relevant customer attribute information.  

The task is to derive meaningful insights from this data and build a model to predict future behaviours.

Instructions:

Consider the following points and use visualisations where appropriate.
•	Generate relevant features that could be valuable for payment behaviour.
•	Conduct a trend analysis on payments over time.
•	Visualize the distribution of companies across different features.
•	Consider what is each customer’s current debt balance, aged debt (>60 days overdue) and whether payment behaviour is improving/declining over time.
•	Assess whether the internal credit rating system is useful.
•	Build a PoC ML model to help proactively identify customers at highest risk of poor payment behaviour. 
•	Evaluate the performance of your model, if any. Which methodology, features and metrics did you choose and why?
